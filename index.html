About Us

The startup heartbeat of GCOE Kolhapur

E-Cell GCOE Kolhapur is the entrepreneurship cell of Government College of Engineering — a student-run powerhouse bridging academic learning with real-world innovation.

From hackathons and pitch fests to competing at IIT Bombay's NEC and placing 2nd Runner-up in both the Basic and Advanced tracks — we prove great ideas come from anywhere.

We build the ecosystem: the mentors, the events, the connections, and the courage for students to think like founders.
